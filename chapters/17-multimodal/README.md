# Chapter 17 — Multimodal — Illustrating the Stories

> Words aren't the end. We give the Storyteller pictures — a tiny image generator to illustrate its tales.

## What you'll learn

We extend beyond text: **VQVAE/VQGAN** to tokenize images into discrete codes (so a Transformer can model them just like text), and the **diffusion Transformer (DiT)** behind modern image generators. We connect it back so the Storyteller can both write *and* draw.

## Key concepts

- images as tokens (VQVAE/VQGAN)
- discrete latent codes
- diffusion models
- the Diffusion Transformer (DiT)
- text-to-image conditioning

## 🔧 Mini-project (planned)

Train a small VQVAE or diffusion model on a simple image set and condition it on your story text, so the Storyteller produces a little illustration to accompany each tale.
